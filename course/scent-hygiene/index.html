The Complete Hygiene Blueprint

A disciplined routine covers every detail, from head to toe.

01

Shower Routine

Shower daily — especially after training. Focus on armpits, groin, and feet. Use quality body wash, exfoliate 1–2 times per week, shampoo 2–3 times, always condition. Do not shower on autopilot. Shower with intention.

02

Oral Hygiene — Non-Negotiable

Bad breath destroys attraction instantly. Brush twice daily, floss every day, use a tongue scraper, and finish with alcohol-free mouthwash. Your breath should stay fresh — never distracting.

03

Face & Beard Maintenance

Cleanse your face morning and night. If you grow a beard, wash it regularly, apply beard oil, comb it daily, and trim it often. A beard should look intentional — never lazy.

04

Hands & Nails

Your hands are seen more than you think. Trim nails weekly, clean underneath daily, and moisturize when needed. Details separate average men from disciplined men.

05

Foot Hygiene

Wash feet thoroughly, dry completely, use powder if needed, and change socks daily. Neglect leads to odor and fungus. Control it before it becomes a problem.

The Scent Strategy

Smelling good is not about spraying more. It is about control, restraint, and consistency.

Deodorant vs. Antiperspirant

Deodorant controls odor. Antiperspirant reduces sweat. Choose based on your body, activity level, and climate.

Application Rules

Apply fragrance to clean skin only. Spray on pulse points — neck, wrists, chest. Keep it to 2–3 sprays maximum. Subtle is powerful.

Scent Etiquette: Never reapply in public. Do not layer multiple strong fragrances. Go lighter in enclosed spaces. Your scent should be discovered — not announced.

Choose Your Signature Scent

Find a scent that feels like you. Consistency builds identity.

Fresh & Clean

Dark & Seductive

Best for evening, mystery, and intensity.
